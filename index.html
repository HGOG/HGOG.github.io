We have daily guided walks and booked walks with different themes. 

Conducted by the City of Hereford Mayor’s Guild of Guides

Daily Walks

Join us to see Hereford’s interesting and historic buildings and other structures, hear about the history of the city through the ages from Saxon times, its role in the Civil War and its famous people.

Booked Walks

The Guild welcomes bookings from clubs, societies and schools. Walks can be arranged at any time of the year with starting and finishing points planned to suit the convenience of the party. 

Virtual Tours

We are also able to offer "Virtual Tours" to groups who may not be able to come on a walk or who would like to book a talk about the history of our city.
